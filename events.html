Ottawa Trip

Are you looking for an internship or a full-time job? Do you want to connect with the engineering industry? ECSESS offers you the opportunity to visit some awesome companies from Ottawa's high tech industry. Companies in the past years includes Shopify, Cisco, Ciena, Alcatel Lucent and more! Usually held in February, come join us and make connections and friends along the way!
