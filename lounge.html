ECSESS Lounge

The ECSESS lounge, located on the first floor of the Trottier bulding is a hangout space for students in electrical, computer, and software engineering to hangout and meet new people. It doubles as the office for the ECSESS council and someone from the council will always be there to answer any questions you may have.

Come by and play some fooseball and piano, or just come hang out on one of the many couches we have! We always love meeting new fellow students so don't be shy and come on in!
